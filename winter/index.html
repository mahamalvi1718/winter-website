ABOUT PAKISTAN

The Northern Areas are the most spectacular and fascinating region of Pakistan.
The three world's famous mountain ranges meet here — the Himalayas,
the Karakorams and the Hindukush. The historic Karakoram Pass (5,575 meters), an ancient trading route between
the Northern Pakistan and Xinjiang, China, gives its name to the range,
which forms the watershed between the Indus and the Central Asian deserts.

Muree

Murree is a picturesque hill station in Pakistan, known for its charming streets, lush greenery, and refreshing climate. It offers a tranquil escape with scenic viewpoints and a peaceful ambiance, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and tourists.

Naran Kaghan

Naran Kaghan is a breathtakingly beautiful valley in Pakistan, renowned for its stunning landscapes, crystal-clear lakes, and majestic mountains. It is a paradise for nature enthusiasts, offering a serene and picturesque escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Azad Jammu and Kashmir

Azad Jammu and Kashmir is a scenic region in northern Pakistan known for its natural beauty. It is also a politically significant area due to its disputed status and border with India.

Pakistan's Northern Areas

Pakistan's northern areas are a breathtaking blend of towering peaks,
lush green valleys, and crystal-clear lakes. This region is a paradise
for adventure enthusiasts, offering opportunities for trekking, mountaineering, and skiing.
